Some things I've built...
Agralogics API and Open Source Platform
I worked with Agralogics to build and launch the first iteration of the Agralogics API, which is the foundation of the Agralogics Open Source Platform. The API delivers high quality and granular agronomic data for any location in the USA. To further support the growers and researchers who leverage our data, we launched an open source repository of agronomic models. These automatically consume our API and compute critical health statistics for growers such as soil conductivity and the onset of various diseases.
TopQ
TopQ is a platform which enables organizations to communicate effectively by hosting large, remote, and real-time meetings for curated Q&A and voting and generating secure documentation. The platform has facilitated over 3000 interactions and hosted events with over 100 participants. We built it in Spring 2020 at the onset of the Covid-19 pandemic, when we realized how difficult and inefficient holding big meetings online was.
